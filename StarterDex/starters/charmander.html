Starter Dex

Charmander

Pros

  • Very strong once it evolves into Charizard.
  • Fire/Flying dual typing adds good coverage.
  • Great offensive stats and movepool.

Cons

  • Rough early game with weak matchups.
  • Low defenses make it easy to KO early on.
  • 4× weakness to Rock as Charizard.
