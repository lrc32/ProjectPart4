Starter Dex

Torchic

Pros

  • Evolves into Blaziken, a powerhouse Fire/Fighting type.
  • Excellent attack stats and strong offensive movepool.
  • Very strong in mid and late game battles.

Cons

  • Weak early game until evolution.
  • Fire/Fighting typing becomes common in later generations.
  • Low defenses make it easy to knock out.
