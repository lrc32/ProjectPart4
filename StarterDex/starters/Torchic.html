Starter Dex

Torchic

Pros

  • Very good in the E4 and Champion battles
  • Has very good attacking stats and a great Hidden Ability
  • Has good coverage moves

Cons

  • Struggles early game and against most Gym Leaders
  • Its Fire type is weak to Water, which is extremely common in Hoenn
  • Low defenses make it easy to KO
