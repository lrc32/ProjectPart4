Starter Dex

Cyndaquil

Pros

  • Supereffective against Bug, Ice, and Steel gyms
  • Good Special Attack and Speed stats
  • Good level-up moveset gives it access to strong fire type moves like Flamethrower and Eruption

Cons

  • Average defenses make it easy to KO
  • Lacks good coverage
  • Struggles in the Champion Battle
