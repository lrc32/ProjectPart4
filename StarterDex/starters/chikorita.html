Starter Dex

Chikorita

Pros

  • Good defensive stats and support moves.
  • Useful moves like Reflect, Light Screen, and PoisonPowder.
  • Reliable for long, strategic battles.

Cons

  • Grass typing is weak to many common types in Johto.
  • Struggles against several early Gym Leaders.
  • Not very strong offensively.
