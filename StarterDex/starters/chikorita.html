Starter Dex

Chikorita

Pros

  • Can stick around thanks to moves like Leech Seed and Synthesis
  • Has access to good support moves like Reflect, Light Screen, and PoisonPowder
  • Good defensive stats

Cons

  • Has bad offensive Coverage
  • Struggles against several Gym Leaders and E4
  • Solo Grass is a bad typing
