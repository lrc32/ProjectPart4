Starter Dex

Squirtle

Pros

  • Supereffective against Rock, Fire, and Ground Gyms
  • Solo Water is a good type
  • Has good coverage moves

Cons

  • Is fairly slow, having the lowest speed stat of the 3
  • Very balanced stats mean it doesn't excel at any particular role
  • There are many alternatives to Blastoise that do its job just as well if not better
