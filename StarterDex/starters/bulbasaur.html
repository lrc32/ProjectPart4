Starter Dex

Bulbasaur

Pros

  • Strong early game
  • Has access to good status moves like: Stun Spore, Poison Powder, and Sleep Powder
  • Supereffective against Rock, Water, and Ground Gyms

Cons

  • Falls off later in the game
  • Lacks good coverage (Non-STAB attacks)
  • Cant do as much consistent damage as it lacks access to more powerful moves
