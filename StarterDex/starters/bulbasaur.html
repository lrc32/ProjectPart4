Starter Dex

Bulbasaur

Pros

  • Strong early game thanks to type advantages.
  • Dual Grass/Poison typing gives useful resistances.
  • Great starter for beginners.

Cons

  • Falls off later in the game.
  • Struggles against common types like Fire and Flying.
  • Not as offensively strong as other starters.
