Starter Dex

Treecko

Pros

  • Strong against early-game Rock and Water types.
  • High Speed makes it easy to strike first.
  • Good movepool with strong Grass attacks.

Cons

  • Very frail compared to other Hoenn starters.
  • Struggles against many Gym Leaders.
  • Grass typing leaves many weaknesses.
