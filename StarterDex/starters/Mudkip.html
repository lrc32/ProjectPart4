Starter Dex

Mudkip

Pros

  • Becomes Water/Ground — only one weakness (Grass).
  • Extremely strong against many Hoenn Gym Leaders.
  • High Attack and solid bulk.

Cons

  • 4× weakness to Grass can be dangerous.
  • Not as fast as other starters.
  • Movepool starts slow early-game.
